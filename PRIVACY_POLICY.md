# Privacy Policy for Markdown Clipper

**Effective Date:** December 24, 2024

## Data Collection

Markdown Clipper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information.

## What the Extension Does

- Converts selected webpage content to Markdown format
- Copies the converted content to your clipboard
- All processing happens locally in your browser
- No data is sent to any external servers

## Permissions Used

- **contextMenus**: To add "Copy as Markdown" to the right-click menu
- **activeTab**: To access the content of the current tab when you use the extension
- **clipboardWrite**: To copy the converted Markdown to your clipboard
- **scripting**: To inject content scripts that perform the conversion

## Data Storage

The extension does not store any data. All conversions are temporary and exist only in memory during the conversion process.

## Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services or analytics.
